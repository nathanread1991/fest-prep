About Festival Playlist Generator

Discover new music through festival lineups and create personalized playlists

What We Do

Festival Playlist Generator helps music lovers discover new artists and create personalized playlists based on festival lineups. By analyzing artist setlists and performance history, we curate the perfect playlist for any festival experience.

How It Works

1. Discover Festivals

Browse upcoming festivals and explore their lineups

2. Analyze Setlists

We analyze artist setlists to find their most popular songs

3. Generate Playlists

Create custom playlists optimized for your music taste

4. Stream Anywhere

Export to Spotify, YouTube Music, and other platforms

Features

  • 🎵 Festival lineup discovery and exploration
  • 🎤 Artist setlist analysis and song recommendations
  • 📱 Mobile-first responsive design
  • 🎧 Integration with major streaming platforms
  • ⚡ Real-time playlist updates
  • 🔍 Advanced search and filtering
  • 👤 Personalized recommendations
  • 📊 Music taste analysis

Technology

Built with modern web technologies including FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis, and React. Our system integrates with Setlist.fm for setlist data and major streaming platforms for playlist creation and management.
