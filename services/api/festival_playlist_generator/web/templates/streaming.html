Connection Help

Why connect streaming services?

Connecting your streaming accounts allows you to:

  • Create playlists directly on your preferred platforms
  • Access your music library anywhere
  • Share playlists with friends
  • Keep your festival preparation organized

Is my data safe?

Yes! We only request the minimum permissions needed to create playlists. We never access your personal information or existing playlists without permission.

Connection issues?

If you're having trouble connecting:

  • Make sure you're logged into your streaming service
  • Check that pop-ups are enabled in your browser
  • Try refreshing the page and connecting again
  • Clear your browser cache if problems persist
